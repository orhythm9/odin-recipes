Creamy Nettle-Potato Soup

Description

A perfect "remedy" for those recovering from illness, where a nourishing, easy-to-digest meal is just what the herbalist ordered.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large yellow onion, chopped
  • 2-3 mediumm potatoes, chopped into small cubes
  • 2 quarts broth (herbal, vegetable, or chicken)
  • Several large handfulls nettle leaf
  • Gratad Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Warm the oil in a large soup pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and saute until soft and golden, about 10 minutes.
  3. Add the potatoes and saute until soft, 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. Add the broth, bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let simmer until the potatoes are almost tender, about 10 minutes.
  5. Add several large handfuls of fresh nettles to the pot.
  6. Cover and let steam until the nettles and potatoes are cooked through, 15-20 minutes.
  7. Puree the soup. Season to taste with the Parmesan, salt and pepper.
